Every few years I rebuild my personal site. This time I wanted to do it differently — to treat the site itself as a project worth documenting, not just a container for other work.

## The brief

The constraints I gave myself:

- No template, no theme. Custom design tokens from the ground up.
- Three themes: Day, Sunset, Night. One CSS custom property swap.
- Built in public. Write about it as I go.
